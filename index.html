Books

The first Harry Potter book, Harry Potter and the Philosopher’s Stone, was

published by Bloomsbury in 1997 to immediate popular and critical acclaim. Six

further best-selling books, three companion books, a playscript and two

screenplays have since followed. The Harry Potter books have been translated

into over 80 languages, won multiple awards, and sold more than 500 million

copies worldwide, becoming the best-selling book series in history..
